Consent

You are asked to participate in a research study conducted at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. You were selected as a possible participant in this study because you are over 21 years old and you started the process of downloading the study app.

Participation and Withdrawal

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you are free to choose whether to be in it or not. If you choose to be in this study, you may subsequently withdraw from it at any time without penalty or consequences of any kind. The investigator may withdraw you from this research if circumstances arise which warrant doing so. You are able to turn on and off data streams within the app that allow you to control your data. You are expected to run the app on your smartphone until the end of the study period, until May, 2017.

Purpose of the Study

The study is designed to help increase awareness of the spread of flu and flu-like symptoms and educate you on how to reduce the risk and prevent infection. Your data along with other users of the app could potentially improve the overall outcome of flu in the general population.

Procedures

- keep the app installed on the phone throughout the duration of the study

Potential Risks and Discomforts

Participating in this study may change how you feel. You may feel more tired, sad, energized, or happy.

Confidentiality
